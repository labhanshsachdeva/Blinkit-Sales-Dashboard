# Blinkit-Sales-Dashboard
1. Project Title

   📊 Blinkit Sales & Outlet Performance Analytics Dashboard

2.Description

This dashboard presents a comprehensive business intelligence solution for analyzing sales performance, product segmentation, and outlet-level efficiency of Blinkit (India’s leading last-minute delivery platform). Built with a focus on clarity and decision support, it transforms raw transactional data into meaningful, actionable insights

3.🔑 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Average Rating: 3.92

Average Sales: 140.99

Total Sales: 1.20M

Total Items: 9,000+

4.📌 Analytical Capabilities

🛒 Product & Category Analysis

Evaluation of sales distribution across item fat content categories (Low Fat, Regular, LF)

Comparative analysis of item types (Fruits, Snacks, Household, Dairy, Frozen, etc.)

Visibility metrics to assess product exposure and its impact on sales performance

🏬 Outlet Performance Insights

Trend analysis of outlet establishment year, highlighting growth patterns over time

Performance comparison based on outlet size (Small, Medium, High)

Detailed breakdown across outlet types, including:

Grocery Stores

Supermarket Type 1, 2, and 3

📈 Data Visualization & Reporting

Interactive visuals including donut charts, bar graphs, and trend lines

Structured tabular reports for precise metric comparison

Clean and intuitive UI for enhanced user experience and quick decision-making

🎯 Business Value

This dashboard enables:

Data-driven decision-making through centralized performance tracking

Identification of high-performing products and outlets

Improved understanding of customer preferences and purchasing patterns

Strategic planning for inventory, marketing, and expansion

🛠️ Technology Stack

Power BI – Data Visualization & Dashboard Design

DAX – Data Modeling & Calculations

Excel / CSV – Data Source Integratio
